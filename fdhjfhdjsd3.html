Room 3


Finally, done with the numbers and Math!...? Maybe :P

It was lucky that you guys remembered the symbol for 'H' from the first room. Now you notice the door to the third room and you enter it. The lanterns light up like clockwork and this time you aren't surprised anymore. You notice a rather long engraving on one of the walls and a huge stone structure with vessels on it. This room is twice as large as the previous ones. You reach towards the stone table and notice that some of the vessels set on it are filled, some empty and some half filled (or half empty :P). You further realize that each vessel has a key in it with digits engraved on them. One of these keys must be the key to entering the next room.



But which one is the right one?
